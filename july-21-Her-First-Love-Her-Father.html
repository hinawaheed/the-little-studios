Her First Love, Her Father

Resource Your Happiness!!

She still has faded memories with him, he was the only person on this planet who used to adore her selflessly, unconditionally. He had always admired her for her righteous deeds and covered all her faults. He never tried to rectify or change her, rather he told her the difference between right and wrong.


She remembers even now, once he told her that there will be always two choices to learn, either by experience or by observation. Both have their significance but in experiencing something she would have more chances of pitfalls or any harm, but through observations, she can be an unharmed learner. As he cherished her to a great extent, he always advised her to be an observant.


Yet she hasn't found that affection she used to acquire from him. Sometimes she recalls her last moments with him, she hated that evening, that environment, and everything she was surrounded by. When he saw her from ten steps far, with his eyes filled with tears she felt her soul ripping out of her body. She knew it was a goodbye look, she loathed herself that she couldn't do anything for the love of her life. She immediately left that place and an hour later she heard the news of his demise. She harks back to that moment when she lost everything.


The next day before his funeral, she captured his fine wrinkled face with white pearled stubble, in her heart. He was looking innocent as a newborn child, she pecked his hand so his sleep couldn't get disturbed. Whatever happens, happens for a good cause. She lost him in her life so she could see him in a better place. May he rest in peace and under the shadow of Almighty's mercy.


Author : Sahar Hamza
