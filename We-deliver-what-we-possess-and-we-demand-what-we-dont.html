Our Build

It's human nature, we deliver what we possess and we demand what we don't. And it's a fact that we receive whatever we give.


When someone shows you hatred and detestation, accept it respectfully in regard to lacking that abhorrence. Do not get mad at them rather offer your commiserations to them, as they are burning themselves into ashes with enviousness and incompetency. They'll gather all negative estimations about you and your nature and will claim you to be fitted in that rotten model made by them. You'll not need to explain anything, as they have already developed their viewpoint about you and that can't be changed with your explanations or facts. So, just agree to them whatever assumptions they impose on you. Because time reveals the truth and precision.


It's the same case with those who seek or try to distribute love and affection. Love is the very core of humanity and itâ€™s the most positive form of emotions if applied in the right way, which means TO GIVE. And we can give only those things which we have at our back ends. Whenever we help, forgive, trust, admire, regard people we exercise benevolence. We shape up ourselves into better beings. For instance, if you ever try to assist a blind man to cross the road, there will be a feeling of contentment in your heart and your day will go positive. On the contrary, if you speak ill to someone, you'll have a feeling of uncertainty and discontentment.


That's why we need to spread love affection in our surroundings to get all positive vibes that are healthy for our peace of mind. And as the earth is round, our emitted aura will definitely get back to us, so we should give only those essentials which we expect to have for ourselves.
